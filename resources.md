## Books (In Recommended Reading Order)

1. **The Little Book of Common Sense Investing** — John Bogle  
   The ETF investment bible. Short, clear, essential.

2. **One Up on Wall Street** — Peter Lynch  
   How to find great stocks in everyday life. Highly readable.

3. **The Psychology of Money** — Morgan Housel  
   Why behavior matters more than knowledge. Read early.

4. **Options as a Strategic Investment** — Lawrence McMillan  
   The reference manual for options. Dense but thorough. Read after Module 04.

5. **The Intelligent Investor** — Benjamin Graham  
   Warren Buffett's bible. Long-term value investing fundamentals.
